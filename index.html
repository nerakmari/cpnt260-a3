Philosophy

Everyone needs a friend!

No matter where you are, who you are, or what you are, you can receive mail!

So What Is Open Mail?

Open Mail is a reconnecting program for folks that are in dire need of someone to talk to. We all know how awkward it is to meet someone, so why not just mail it?

But Why Mail?

Well, we're bringing back the old school way of networking which is more intimate and authentic.
